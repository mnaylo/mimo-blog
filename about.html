Our Story

CyberGuard is a cybersecurity services and consulting firm intent on disrupting the industry. We accomplish this by taking a collaborative and transformative approach, with a “cloud-first” focus.

Cloud providers operate in a Shared Responsibility Model. They are responsible for security of the cloud, organizations remain responsible for the security of their data in the cloud. This requires a new approach by security professionals and businesses alike, to gain visibility and understanding of their security posture in a world of cloud services.

We believe that a well-designed information security strategy, leveraging cloud where appropriate is an enabler for businesses to rapidly and effectively reach cybersecurity maturity and to institute the most impactful risk management program possible.
